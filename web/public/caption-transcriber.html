Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is caption transcription?

CaptiOCR achieves high accuracy when transcribing captions because it captures the already-processed text displayed on your screen, rather than trying to interpret audio. It's not affected by accents or background noise.

Which platforms are supported?

CaptiOCR works with any platform that displays captions on your screen, including Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, Webex, GoToMeeting, and others. It's universally compatible.

How is speaker identification handled?

CaptiOCR captures speaker information exactly as it appears in the captions. Most platforms include the speaker's name before their text, which CaptiOCR preserves in its transcription.

Is my meeting content secure?

Absolutely. CaptiOCR processes everything locally on your device. No audio, video, or text content is ever sent to external servers, making it ideal for confidential discussions.

Can I edit the transcriptions?

Yes, CaptiOCR saves transcripts as plain text files that you can open and edit with any text editor. This makes it easy to clean up any minor errors or format the content.

What languages are supported?

CaptiOCR currently supports caption transcription in English, Italian, French, Spanish, German, and Portuguese. Additional languages will be added in future updates.
