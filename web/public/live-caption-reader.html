Frequently Asked Questions

How accurately does CaptiOCR read captions?

CaptiOCR achieves high accuracy when reading on-screen captions because it captures the actual text displayed. It works with both automated and human-generated captions across all platforms.

Can it read captions from pre-recorded videos?

Yes, CaptiOCR works with any visible captions, including those in pre-recorded videos, webinars, or online course materials, as long as the captions are displayed on your screen.

Does it work with all video conferencing platforms?

Yes, CaptiOCR works with any platform that displays captions on screen, including Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, GoToMeeting, educational platforms, and streaming services.

How does it handle fast-changing captions?

CaptiOCR continuously monitors the caption area and captures text several times per second. This ensures it can read and record even rapidly changing captions that would be impossible to copy manually.

Is my meeting content secure?

Absolutely. CaptiOCR processes everything locally on your device. No caption text is ever sent to external servers, making it suitable for confidential meetings, proprietary courses, and sensitive discussions.

What languages are supported?

CaptiOCR currently supports reading captions in English, Italian, French, Spanish, German, and Portuguese. This makes it ideal for international meetings and language learning environments.
