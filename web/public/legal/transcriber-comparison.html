The Privacy Revolution in Meeting Transcription

Effective Date: March 2025

While AI transcription services like Granola, Otter.ai, and Fireflies offer powerful features, they come with a hidden cost: your privacy. These services send your confidential meeting data to external AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) for processing, store recordings in the cloud, and some even use your conversations to train their AI models.

CaptiOCR represents a fundamental shift toward privacy-first transcription. By extracting text directly from your screen using local OCR technology, CaptiOCR ensures that your sensitive business discussions, client meetings, and personal conversations never leave your device. No external servers, no AI training on your data, no monthly subscription fees.

Comparison between CaptiOCR and Classical Transcription Assistants

The Fundamental Difference: Audio Recording vs Screen OCR

All major meeting transcription services - including "privacy-focused" ones like Jamie AI - require recording your audio in some form. Whether through a visible bot (Otter.ai, Fireflies) or system-level audio capture (Jamie, Granola), they all need to process your actual conversations. This creates an inherent trust requirement: you must trust them to handle, process, and delete your audio recordings properly.

CaptiOCR operates on a fundamentally different principle: it reads only the text already displayed on your screen (live captions), never recording any audio. This eliminates the trust layer entirely - there's simply no audio data to worry about.

The Critical Distinction: Recording vs Reading

Why CaptiOCR's Approach is Fundamentally Different:

  • Audio Recorders (All Competitors): Capture your voice → Upload/Process audio → Promise to delete → You must trust them
  • Screen OCR (CaptiOCR Only): Read visible text → Process locally → Nothing to delete → No trust required

Even the most "privacy-focused" competitors like Jamie AI still record your system audio, upload it to servers (even if in the EU), and process it before deletion. This creates multiple points where your sensitive conversations could be exposed, leaked, or mishandled - as demonstrated by Granola's 333 exposed transcripts and Otter.ai's privacy lawsuits.

CaptiOCR eliminates this entire attack surface by never recording audio in the first place. It simply reads the text already visible on your screen - the same text you can see with your own eyes.

Why CaptiOCR Leads in Privacy and Security

  • Zero External Data Processing: Unlike Granola (sends data to OpenAI/Anthropic) or Otter.ai (uses recordings for training), CaptiOCR processes everything locally on your device. Your meeting content never leaves your computer.

  • No Bot Disruption: Extracts text directly from screen without joining meetings as a bot, maintaining natural meeting flow.
