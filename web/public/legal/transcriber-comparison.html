The Privacy Revolution in Meeting Transcription

Effective Date: March 2025

While AI transcription services like Granola, Otter.ai, and Fireflies offer powerful features, they come with a hidden cost: your privacy. These services send your confidential meeting data to external AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) for processing, store recordings in the cloud, and some even use your conversations to train their AI models.

CaptiOCR represents a fundamental shift toward privacy-first transcription. By extracting text directly from your screen using local OCR technology, CaptiOCR ensures that your sensitive business discussions, client meetings, and personal conversations never leave your device. No external servers, no AI training on your data, no monthly subscription fees.

Why CaptiOCR Leads in Privacy and Security

  • Zero External Data Processing: Unlike Granola (sends data to OpenAI/Anthropic) or Otter.ai (uses recordings for training), CaptiOCR processes everything locally on your device. Your meeting content never leaves your computer.
  • No Cloud Dependencies: Works completely offline - no internet required for core functionality, unlike cloud-dependent competitors.
  • No Bot Disruption: Extracts text directly from screen without joining meetings as a bot, maintaining natural meeting flow.
  • Complete User Control: You own and control all your data locally - no external storage, no third-party access, no data breaches possible.
  • Enterprise-Ready Privacy: Perfect for sensitive business meetings where competitors like Granola (333 transcripts exposed in 2025) or Otter.ai (multiple lawsuits) pose compliance risks.
  • Free & Open Source: No subscription fees ($10-24/month for competitors) and full transparency of code - you can verify exactly how your data is handled.
  • Future-Proof AI Integration: Optional AI processing can be added later in controlled environments or through local models - always user's choice, never mandatory.

  • Government & Defense: Classified or sensitive government communications

Conclusion

While AI-powered transcription services offer advanced features, they come with significant privacy trade-offs. CaptiOCR represents a fundamental shift toward user-controlled, privacy-first transcription technology. By keeping all data processing local, CaptiOCR eliminates the risks of data breaches, unauthorized AI training, and compliance violations that plague cloud-based alternatives.

Choose CaptiOCR when privacy, security, and user control matter more than AI-generated summaries that require sending your confidential conversations to external servers. Your data deserves to stay where it belongs - with you.
